Salmon Cookies

We bake fresh Salmon Cookies everyday. We have stores in Seattle, Bellevue, Redmond, & Kent.

A salmon cookie is a baked or cooked food that is small, flat and sweet. It usually contains flour, sugar, salmon flesh and some type of oil or fat. It may include other ingredients such as raisins, oats, chocolate chips, nuts, etc. In most English-speaking countries except for the United States and Canada, crisp cookies are called biscuits. Chewier biscuits are sometimes called cookies even in the United Kingdom. Some cookies may also be named by their shape, such as date squares or bars.
